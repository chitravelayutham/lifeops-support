Common questions

A few things people often ask.

Is my data really private?

Yes. Your document files are stored on your device only, encrypted with a key based on your master password. Nothing is synced to a cloud or server, and no one else — including me — can read them.

What happens if I forget my master password?

When you first set up LifeOps, you're given a one-time recovery key. That key is the only way to regain access if you forget your password. Because everything is encrypted on your device with no cloud backup, please keep your recovery key somewhere safe — if both the password and the recovery key are lost, your vault can't be recovered. That's the trade-off for true privacy.

How do reminders work?

Add a document's expiry date or a deadline, and LifeOps reminds you at 30, 7, and 1 day before. You control the timing.

Does it need an internet connection?

No — LifeOps works fully offline. Everything runs on your device.

Do I need an account?

No account, no email sign-up, no login. You just set a master password on first launch.

Can I back up or move my data to a new device?

Yes. You can export an encrypted backup and restore it — and only you, with your password, can open it.

How does the AI assistant work?

You can ask things like "What's due this week?" It answers entirely on your device, with zero network calls — your questions never leave your phone.

Is LifeOps free?

Yes, LifeOps is free.
