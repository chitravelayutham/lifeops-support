Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

LifeOps is built on one principle: your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.
🔒

Data Storage

Everything you enter — documents, obligations, and subscriptions — is encrypted and stored locally on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption. Nothing is sent to any server or cloud service.

👤

Biometrics

Face ID and Touch ID are handled entirely by iOS. LifeOps never accesses, processes, or stores your biometric data.

🤖

AI Assistant

The AI Assistant runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Foundation Models. Your queries and data never leave your device. On devices without Apple Intelligence, the app uses a built-in offline engine. The AI makes zero network calls.

💾

Backup and Restore

Backup exports an encrypted .lox file to your local Files app using AES-256-GCM encryption with Argon2id key derivation. The file is protected by your master password and never uploaded anywhere. Restore reads the file directly from your device.

🌐

Network

LifeOps makes one anonymous network request at launch to check for emergency feature flags (https://flags.lifeops.app/v1/kill). No user data, device identifiers, or personal information is included in this request. The app functions normally if this endpoint is unreachable.

📊

Analytics

LifeOps contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We have no idea how you use the app — by design.

✉️

Contact
