Why LifeOps exists

It started with folders. Stacks of them. Tax documents, insurance, passports, immigration papers, the subscriptions you forget you're paying for — the important paperwork of a life, scattered across your head, your inbox, a sticky note, and a box in the closet.

Miss one date and it costs you money, time, or a stressful week. So I built an app I wish I had.

And I built it on one line I wasn't willing to cross: your documents never leave your device. Encrypted, on your phone, readable only by you — not on a server, not in the cloud, not even visible to me. That promise shaped everything else about the app.

Encrypted Vault

Your documents. Your device.

Store passports, leases, insurance, and more. Every file is encrypted on your device and never leaves it. Not on any server. Not in any cloud. Not visible to anyone — including us.

Never Miss a Renewal

Reminded before anything expires.

LifeOps tracks every expiry date and reminds you at 30, 7, and 1 day before. Passport, licence, insurance, lease — you set the timing, you stay ahead.

Subscriptions

Stop paying for things you forgot.

Track every subscription, its renewal date, and your total monthly spend — so you decide, not your bank. No bank connection required. You enter what you pay.

On-device AI

Ask. It answers. Nothing leaves your device.

“What’s due this week?” “Which documents are expiring?” The built-in assistant reasons across your obligations, documents, and subscriptions — processed entirely on your device, with zero network calls. Your questions never leave your phone.

The one promise that never changes

Your documents never leave your device.

No account. No cloud. No servers holding your files. Nothing is synced, nothing is shared, and you can erase everything instantly. No one else is in control — just you.

Encrypted on-device

Files are protected with AES-256 encryption and a master password only you know. There is no cloud copy.

No account, no reset

No email, no server login. Only you and your recovery key can open your vault — not even us.

Works fully offline

Everything runs on your device, so it keeps working with no connection and nothing to leak.
